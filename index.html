Akkadu?

Thousands of years ago, in the
middle east there was a city called
Babylon where the citizens where
building a very tall tower with the goal
to reach heaven, the Babel Tower
But something happened...
Ancient stories say that the workers
were not able to finish the tower
because started to talk different
languages and
couldn’t communicate anymore.
The original language, the one
they forgot how to speak, was called
Akkadian. In honor of that forgotten
language we call ourselves Akkadu,
which in Akkadian means
“coming from Akkadian”.
